# RAVAND OS

## Vision

RAVAND OS is an AI Business Operating System designed for engineering companies, fabrication labs, industrial design studios and manufacturing businesses.

The goal is to create an intelligent digital employee capable of:

- Managing customers
- Managing sales
- Creating marketing content
- Answering clients
- Managing projects
- Managing reminders
- Managing company knowledge
- Monitoring industry news
- Analyzing competitors
- Managing engineering workflows
- Supporting executive decision making

The system must be modular.

Every capability must be implemented as an independent AI Agent.

The platform must support both Local AI models and Cloud AI models.

The architecture should be scalable and suitable for commercial deployment.
